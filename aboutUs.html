Our Approach

Our goal was to create a natural essential oil-based bug repellent while keeping it safe, natural, and non-toxic. Living in Texas gives our families great opportunities for fun and outdoor adventures. Nature has provided a solution for us to enjoy the outdoors without using harmful chemicals. Our product is based on natural essential oils and botanicals.

We recommend that you apply No Buggedy directly to your clothing and gear before your next outing. It is also recommended that you spray your luggage and belongings when traveling and staying in hotels. As with any new product, please test it on a small area of skin to check for allergies.

Our products are safe for your entire family, including children and pets.

Founder

Our founder is a retired Army Veteran with 24 years of service to her country. NoBuggedy was an extention of her vision and dedication to make natural products to serve our community.
