Through testing and research, we created a natural solution that will protect your loved ones from bugs and the diseases they carry.

No Buggedy is made of natural essential oils that repel many of the pests that attack us in and outside of our homes.

Our products have proven to be effective in repelling the following insects:

  • Bed Bugs
  • Chiggers

  • No See Ums "Ceratopogonidae"

How to use the product

We recommend you apply No Buggedy directly to your clothing and gear before your next outing.

Spraying your luggage and belongings when traveling and staying in hotels.

As with any new product, please test it on a small area of skin to check for allergies. Our products are safe for children and pets, but please avoid applying near eyes or mouth.

For additional protection, you can spray your pets bedding and other places they may sleep.
